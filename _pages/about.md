I am a first-year PhD student in Data Science at UC San Diego's Halıcıoğlu Data Science Institute, 
supported by [The Hartwell Foundation](https://thehartwellfoundation.org/). I received my B.S. in 
Data Science, Mathematics, and Economics (Provost Honor) from UC San Diego in March 2025.

My research focuses on **statistical genomics and computational biology**, with an emphasis on 
genetic risk prediction for complex diseases. I lead the construction of the largest meta-analysis 
GWAS for childhood-onset asthma (N > 1.3M) and develop integrated frameworks combining 
Polygenic Risk Scores (PRS) and Polygenic Transcriptome Risk Scores (PTRS) to better predict 
disease risk.

I am also interested in **genomic foundation models**, where I develop biologically informed DNA 
tokenization schemes and deep learning pipelines for gene expression prediction.

Previously, I conducted research at the [UC Institute on Global Conflict and Cooperation](https://igcc.ucsd.edu/), 
the [Power Transformation Lab](https://ptl.ucsd.edu/), and the UCSD Department of Economics.
